The EIF provides principles and layers for achieving interoperability across EU public sector systems.

## Core Principles
- Openness  
- Transparency  
- Reusability  
- Technological neutrality  
- Administrative simplification  

## Interoperability Layers
1. **Legal interoperability**  
2. **Organisational interoperability**  
3. **Semantic interoperability**  
4. **Technical interoperability**

## Notes
These concepts guide cross-country digital projects and allow systems to exchange data consistently and securely.
